During my time in New York, I came to the realization that my true passion lies in the field of software engineering.

I am proficient in JavaScript, Python, Node.js, HTML5, CSS, React, Express, Redux, Webpack, Babel, Jquery, API, PostgreSQL, Three.js, D3, React, Bootstrap, Heroku, Git, and knowledgeable with Github and Figma. Through collaborative teamwork, we were able to make a positive impact and contribute towards the betterment of our community.

Overall, my passion for software engineering has led me on an exciting career pivot, and I am eager to see where my skills and knowledge will take me next.
